About HomeHealthWare
HomeHealthWare is a turn-key electronic record management system designed for companies providing services in the home health industry.

Homehealthware handles:
  • Client records
  • Client care plans
  • Employee records
  • Employee scheduling and routing
  • Employee time tracking
  • Electronic Visit Verification (EVV)
  • MCO/CCU/PvtPay billing
  • Advanced reporting
